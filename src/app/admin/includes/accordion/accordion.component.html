Technical Project Lead (India)
ID 2022-3212

Overview

Do you thrive in a fast paced environment and embrace change? Do you have a passion for customer service and building robust relationships with your clients? Do you enjoy strategic planning and developing innovative solutions to successfully guide software development projects across the finish line? As a Project Lead on our implementation team, you will have the opportunity to manage cutting edge software development projects and lead multiple project teams to victory!

Responsibilities

If you have utilized 30% of the software development project budget, has 30% of the software development been completed? This is a question that you will analyze and solve on a daily basis.


A day in the life of a Project Lead at SOLTECH includes guiding teams of software engineers through complex, custom software builds and ensuring that development aligns with client needs. You will be responsible for ensuring that the project stays in line with budget and that resources are used at full capacity at all times while not going over budget. You will provide regular demos of progress to external clients and deliver reports to go over completed tasks and status of budget.


You will demonstrate exceptional professionalism and critical thinking skills at all times. You will be a proactive problem solver and look at problems ahead of you, vs. identifying the problem in the rear-view. You will be nimble and unafraid of change; always thinking on your toes!

Developing relationships with clients and earning their trust is the key to success. You will exhibit superb customer service skills and maintain an account management mindset; always looking for areas to earn new business and enhance the relationship that we have with our client.


Qualifications

Why would we be interested in you? Because you have:

  • Extensive experience in an external customer facing role with a high volume of communication
  • Passion for providing exceptional customer service
  • Proven ability to communicate complex technical problems and solutions to a non technical audience
  • Experience managing software development projects from start to finish, while monitoring financial budget and scope
  • Robust experience managing multiple tasks at once and seeing them through to completion
  • Intuitive abilities to generate long term and short term strategic plans for monitoring scope creep and delivering within a pre-set estimate
  • Experience working within project management systems including SmartSheet, Microsoft Project, and JIRA
  • Knowledge of the full cycle of software development from start to finish
  • Experience in a consulting environment (bonus points!)
